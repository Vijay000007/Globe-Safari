🌐 Travel Tips for Smart Explorers

  • 📱 Always carry a power bank & universal adapter — especially for long layovers or remote trips.
  • 💳 Use a forex card or international debit card — avoid ATM fees & carry less cash.
  • 🛂 Scan your documents — store passports/visas online securely on Google Drive or Dropbox.
  • 🧳 Pack light & smart — take layers, not weight. Roll clothes to save space.
  • 📡 Get a local SIM or eSIM — stay connected and navigate with Google Maps or Rome2Rio.
  • 📆 Book at least 60 days in advance for best rates on flights and visa processing timelines.
  • ✅ Don’t skip travel insurance — especially in Europe, Thailand, or Vietnam.
